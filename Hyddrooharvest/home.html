About Us

Who we are

We are a sustainable agricultural initiative addressing
food security in rural Kenya by partnering with
smallholder farmers affected by climate change, erratic
rainfall, and degraded soil. Our approach focuses on empowering farmers through capacity building, soil management, water harvesting and precision training.

Learn More

Our Mission

At Hyddroo Harvest, our mission is to cultivate a resilient
and sustainable future for smallholder farmers in rural
Kenya by empowering them with resources and
financial support.
Committed to combating food insecurity exacerbated by
climate change, erratic rainfall, and degraded soil, we strive
to break the cycle of vulnerability for 1 Million
smallholder farmers by 2030.
